About me

I am Kokou Wolali Kpadenou, a frontend developer passionate about modern web development and crafting. I am a US citizen living in Gaithersburg, MD and from TOGO (Country in West Africa). As ‘Forever student’, I am eager to build on my academic foundations in Information Technology and stay in tune with the latest Web technology through continued online courses.


My only and valuable tool is determination, I love making things happen in the positive ways. This mindset gave me the ability to overcome challenges I faced in my previous professional live and recently in my personal life, moving from my country to USA. I believe teamwork is key to success for any organisation, a principle I promote in my last workspaces.


I am currently working as a freelance Web Developer and is always interested in a new challenge. Reach out to kokou2kpadenou@gmail.com to connect.
